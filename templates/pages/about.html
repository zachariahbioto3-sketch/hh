About the association

A student body built on solidarity and sight.

The Kaimosi Friends University Optometry Students' Association (KAFUOSA) represents every student in the Department of Optometry and Vision Science — in the classroom, the clinic, and the community.

Motto

Eyes To The Future

Vision

To offer the best vision and visual environment to humanity through optometry and vision science.

Mission

To fulfill the vision and eye care needs of the public through clinical care, research and education — enhancing quality of life.

Our history.

From the constitution's founding preamble.

KAFUOSA was established by the students of Optometry and Vision Science who recognised the strength that comes from unity. Operating within the authority of departmental regulations, the Kaimosi Friends University Act and statutes, and the laws of the Republic of Kenya, the association exists to guarantee the continued, peaceful representation of every student in the department — bound together by a constitution that all members respect as their guiding document.

What the association does.

As set out in our constitution's aims and objectives.

Liaison

Serving as the official bridge between students, the department, faculty and university staff.

Harmony

Promoting harmony and mutual respect among students, faculty and staff.

Welcoming

Leading the yearly welcome of incoming first-year Optometry students into the department.

Leadership structure.

Elected annually by secret ballot, at the start of each academic year.

01Chairperson
02Vice Chairperson
03Secretary General
04Organizing Secretary
05Treasurer
06Academic Representative
07Class Representatives (male & female)

The association is also supported by a Patron, appointed by the Chairperson of the Optometry Department, who serves as a resource person and channel of communication between KAFUOSA and the university.
